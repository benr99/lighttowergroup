# Light Tower Group Copy Overhaul Strategy

## Commercial objective

Increase qualified conversations with commercial real estate sponsors, developers, and operators evaluating debt, equity, or recapitalization needs of $5M or more.

## Positioning

Light Tower Group is a principal-led capital advisor for commercial real estate sponsors facing consequential capital decisions. Its differentiator is not indiscriminate lender outreach; it is solving the capital-structure and underwriting questions before a deal enters the market.

**Brand principle:** Structure first. Source second.

## Message hierarchy

1. A capital process can fail even when the asset or business plan is compelling.
2. The sponsor needs to resolve the structure before they expose the opportunity to the market.
3. LTG diagnoses the structure, prepares the underwriting case, targets relevant capital providers, and stays engaged through funding.
4. Direct principal involvement creates continuity and accountability from assessment through closing coordination.

## Priority sponsor situations

- A maturity, refinance, or replacement loan no longer fits the asset or business plan.
- Senior proceeds do not complete the capital stack.
- Construction or redevelopment financing must reconcile debt, equity, reserves, recourse, and takeout.
- A bridge loan requires a credible path to the next capital event.
- A sponsor is weighing JV equity, preferred equity, mezzanine, or co-GP alternatives.
- A stabilized asset requires a decision among permanent-debt paths.

## Voice rules

- Lead with the sponsor's decision, downside, and timing—not a generic firm description.
- Use precise capital-markets language, calm authority, and clear qualification.
- Demonstrate institutional rigor through process and evidence; do not use the word as a substitute for proof.
- Avoid guarantees, unsupported market claims, inflated network counts, and generic luxury language.
- Use buyer-facing CTAs: “Request a Confidential Capital Review” or “Request Review.”

## Proof strategy

Public proof should be specific, internally consistent, and limited to approved facts. Representative transactions should explain the capital need and execution context, while preserving confidentiality. Additional details can be shared only with qualified parties under NDA.

## Conversion promise

The first conversation is a confidential assessment of the asset, capital need, timeline, and capital-structure questions. The immediate goal is to determine whether a targeted capital process is appropriate—not to force every prospect into outreach.
