Trammell Crow's Soho Tower Shows Development Is Alive Only for Those With Cheap Land and Patient Capital

The most important number in Trammell Crow's 130 Lafayette Street project is not the 99 rental units or the 273-foot height. It is the $14.7 million land basis secured through a 99-year ground lease. That number is why this building is rising past the halfway mark in mid-2026.
