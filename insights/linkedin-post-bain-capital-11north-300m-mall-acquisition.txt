On Wednesday, Bain Capital and 11North Partners closed a $300 million acquisition of five open-air malls across four states. The portfolio spans 757,000 square feet. Properties include the Beacon in Carlsbad, Calif.; Barcroft Plaza in Falls Church, Va.; West Town Corners in Altamonte Springs, Fla.; and University Commons in Sugar Land, Texas. University Commons was sold by Vista Private Equity Group. Bain Capital is a multi-asset alternative investment firm with $185 billion in assets under management. 11North Partners is a New York-based real estate investment firm focused on retail. The two formed an exclusive joint venture for this transaction.
