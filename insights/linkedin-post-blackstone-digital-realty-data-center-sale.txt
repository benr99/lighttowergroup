Blackstone's $3.5B Data Center Sale Shows the Exit, Not the Thesis

The most important number in Blackstone's $3.5 billion sale of three Northern Virginia data centers to Digital Realty is not the price. It is the consideration mix: $1.2 billion in cash and $2.3 billion in Digital Realty stock. Blackstone is not exiting data centers. It is.
