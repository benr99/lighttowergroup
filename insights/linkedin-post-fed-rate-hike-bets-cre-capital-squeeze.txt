Fed Rate-Hike Bets Signal CRE's Next Capital Squeeze

The most dangerous number in the fed funds market this week is not the rate itself. It is the wager that the rate will move at all. Traders are ramping up bets that the Federal Reserve will raise rates as soon as July, a trade that was unthinkable just months ago.
