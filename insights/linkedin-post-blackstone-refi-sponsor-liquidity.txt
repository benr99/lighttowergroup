Blackstone's $125M Refi Shows Private Credit Is Choosing Sponsors, Not Assets

The most important number in Harbor Group International's $124.6 million refinancing of Alesio Urban Center is not the loan amount. It is the lender. Blackstone provided the capital. That matters because Blackstone is not in the business of making loans it expects to go bad.
