The buyer: Mori Building, the Japanese developer that has co-invested with SL Green on One Vanderbilt and 245 Park Avenue. The property: 346 Madison Avenue, a 46-story all-electric office tower that will rise from Brooks Brothers' former flagship store. The deal values the 850,000-square-foot project at $175 million. SL Green bought the site for $160 million last year. That $15 million markup over twelve months is not a flip. It is a signal: institutional capital sees a path to rent growth in the highest-quality office product, even as the broader market struggles with vacancy and repricing.
