CMBS Delinquency Rate Dips, But the Underlying Pressure Is Not Easing

The headline number is a 20-basis-point decline in the CMBS delinquency rate to 7.35%. The real story is what the aggregate masks: three of five major property types saw their delinquency rates rise, and the cure that drove the improvement came from a single large lodging.
