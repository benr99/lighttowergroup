A $38M Power Center Refi Shows Retail Debt Is Available for the Right Sponsor and Basis

The most important detail in Gantry's $38 million refinancing of Mesa Grand is not the loan amount. It is the structure: full-term interest-only payments with cash-out proceeds, provided by an institutional balance sheet lender. That combination does not appear in a market.
