Google's 600K SF Lease in Chicago: A Tenant Anchor, Not a Market Cure

The most important number in Google's expected 600,000-square-foot lease at Chicago's redeveloped Thompson Center is not the square footage. It is the 354,000 square feet that Google will not occupy. The tech giant is taking roughly 63% of the 954,000-square-foot building.
