On May 28, 2026, a joint venture between DLC Management Corp. and DRA Advisors closed on Shadow Lake Towne Center, a 640,327-square-foot regional shopping center in Papillion, Nebraska, for $95 million. The seller was PREP Property Group. JLL Capital Markets represented the seller. Shadow Lake Towne Center sits on 83.5 acres and was 89 percent occupied at closing. Tenants include Dick's Sporting Goods, JCPenney, T.J. Maxx, Burlington, HomeGoods, Michaels, and Ross Dress for Less. A Hy-Vee grocery store on the site was excluded from the sale.
