Trammell Crow's Soho Tower Shows Development Is Alive Only for Those With Cheap Land and Patience

The most important number in Trammell Crow's 130 Lafayette Street project is not the 99 rental units. It is the 99-year ground lease. A developer building a 25-story tower on land it does not own, with a completion date in Q1 2028, is not making a bet on next year's rent growth.
