Manhattan Sublease Shrinkage Is a Landlord Power Move, Not Just AI Demand

The most important number in the Manhattan office market is not the 719,200 square feet AI firms have leased this year. It is the 12 million square feet of sublease space that has vanished since late 2022. Sublease inventory fell below 11 million square feet in the second.
