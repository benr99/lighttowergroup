NYC Luxury Condo Sales Surge, but the Market Is Starving for Supply, Not Demand

The most revealing number in the second-quarter Manhattan new development data is not the 38 contracts signed above $10 million. It is the 112 new units that launched. That is the lowest quarterly supply injection in a decade. And it explains why the headline—luxury is on.
