CMBS Distress Rate Hits 11.86%: The Resolution Machine Is Not Keeping Pace

The most important number in the May CMBS distress data is not the 11.86 percent headline. It is the 170-basis-point gap between the special servicing rate and the delinquency rate. That spread means a meaningful share of distressed balance is being worked out before it.
