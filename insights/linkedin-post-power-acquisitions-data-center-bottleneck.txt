DigitalBridge's $1.1B ArcLight Deal Shows Power, Not Real Estate, Is Now the Data Center Bottleneck

The most important number in DigitalBridge's $1.1 billion acquisition of ArcLight is not the price. It is the bottleneck it is designed to bypass. Data center developers are no longer constrained by land, zoning, or construction costs. They are constrained by power. The grid.
