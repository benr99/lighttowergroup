Apollo CREF's Dissolution Shows Public REITs Can't Absorb Concentrated Losses

The most important number in Apollo Commercial Real Estate Finance's dissolution is not the $9 billion portfolio sale to Athene. It is the $82 million write-off on a single mezzanine loan at 111 West 57th Street. That one position did not kill the REIT.
