Boston's Empty Lab Towers Show Speculative Capital Has a New Timeline

The most important number in Boston's lab market is not 32.7 percent vacancy. It is $540 million. That is the construction financing PGIM Real Estate and Wheelock Street Capital provided for 10 World Trade, a 570,000-square-foot spec lab tower that delivered in the Seaport.
