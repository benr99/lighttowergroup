Greystar's $3B Fund Target Shows Multifamily Capital Is Concentrating, Not Expanding

The most important number in Greystar's latest fundraise is not the $3 billion target. It is the $1.5 billion already raised in roughly six months. That pace tells the market something specific: institutional limited partners are not broadly reallocating to multifamily.
