The seed portfolio spans nine U.S. markets and includes approximately 53 megawatts of installed power capacity and 259,000 square feet of available colocation space. The acquisition launches a new U.S. data center operating platform targeting colocation, high-density deployments, and artificial intelligence inference infrastructure. I Squared has committed up to $1 billion to build the platform through capital investment, customer-led expansion, and additional acquisitions.
