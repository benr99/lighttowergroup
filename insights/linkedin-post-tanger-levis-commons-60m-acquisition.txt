On May 28, 2026, Tanger closed on a $60 million acquisition of The Town Center at Levis Commons, a 300,000-square-foot mixed-use development in Perrysburg, Ohio. The seller: Hill Partners, the Charlotte-based developer that had owned and managed the property since its 2004 opening. The price works out to $200 per square foot for a center that includes 60-plus retailers, a Cinemark theater, and 69,000 square feet of office space. Tenants include Sephora, Shake Shack, Anthropologie, lululemon, J. Crew Factory, Athleta, and Arhaus.
