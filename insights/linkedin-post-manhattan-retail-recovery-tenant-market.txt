Manhattan Retail’s Recovery Is Real, But It’s a Tenant’s Market, Not a Landlord’s

The most telling number in REBNY’s first-quarter Manhattan retail report is not the average asking rent on Herald Square, which rose from $383 to $412 per square foot. It is the single tiny storefront available on Bleecker Street between Seventh Avenue South and Hudson.
