Privacy

Your inquiry stays focused on your inquiry.

This notice explains what the Light Tower Group website collects, why it is used, and the choices available to you.

Effective July 20, 2026. Light Tower Group uses information submitted through this website only to evaluate, respond to, and administer business inquiries; maintain website security; and meet applicable legal obligations.

Information you provide

The confidential review form may collect your name, company, email address, phone number, capital need, asset type, approximate deal size, and the description you choose to provide. The chat experience may collect the text you enter and related conversation context.

Information collected for security and operation

The hosting platform may process standard technical records such as IP address, browser information, request time, requested page, and error details. The review endpoint uses an IP address temporarily to limit abusive request volume. Do not submit passwords, bank account numbers, tax identifiers, or other sensitive personal information through the form or chat.

How information is used

  • To assess whether an inquiry may fit Light Tower Group’s services.
  • To respond, schedule a conversation, and coordinate follow-up.
  • To protect the website and its forms from abuse.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, or recordkeeping obligations when applicable.

Service providers and disclosure

The website is hosted on Netlify, and form notifications are delivered through Resend. Those providers process information on Light Tower Group’s behalf under their own security and privacy terms. Information is not sold. It may be disclosed when required by law, to protect legal rights or security, or in connection with a business reorganization.

Retention and security

Inquiry information is kept only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes above and any applicable legal obligations. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices

You may ask to access, correct, or delete information you submitted, subject to applicable law and legitimate recordkeeping needs. You may also choose to contact the firm by phone instead of using the website form.

Contact

Questions or requests can be sent to ben@lighttowergroup.co or made by phone at (347) 554-0093.

This notice may be updated when the website’s practices or service providers change. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are published.
