The Mandate

The capital structure is
the deal.

A deal can be right on paper and still fail in the capital markets. A maturity can outpace the refinancing plan. Senior proceeds can fall short. A construction stack can look complete until the lender tests the assumptions.

Light Tower Group helps sponsors solve those questions before a process begins. We assess the asset, business plan, capital structure, and timing—then define the capital strategy most likely to withstand underwriting and execute.

Whether you are refinancing, acquiring, building, recapitalizing, or closing an equity gap, the objective is the same: present an executable opportunity to the capital providers most relevant to the assignment and manage the process through funding.

"The right lender is an outcome of the right structure—not a starting assumption."

Capital Pressure Points

Where a standard financing process breaks down.

01 — Maturity

The existing debt no longer fits.

A looming maturity, reduced proceeds, or a changed credit environment can turn a routine refinance into a capital-structure decision.

02 — Capital Stack

The sources do not yet add up.

Senior debt, equity, preferred capital, recourse, reserves, and timing must work together before the market will take the opportunity seriously.

03 — Execution

A good term sheet is not a closing.

The right process anticipates underwriting questions, screens for realistic fit, and stays active when diligence turns a headline into a real commitment.

04 — Control

Capital changes the economics of the deal.

A sponsor needs to understand the trade-offs among leverage, cost, governance, dilution, promote, and flexibility before selecting a path.

The Advantage

What changes when the structure is solved before the process starts.

01 — Reach

A targeted process—not indiscriminate outreach.

The objective is not to circulate a deal as widely as possible. It is to identify the capital providers whose current mandate, asset appetite, leverage range, and timeline give the assignment a real path to execution.

02 — Precision

Underwriting before marketing.

We pressure-test the story, sizing, structure, and likely diligence questions before introducing the opportunity. Better preparation produces a clearer process and fewer avoidable surprises later.

03 — Alignment

Direct accountability from strategy through funding.

Benjamin Rohr is directly involved from the initial assessment through lender and investor dialogue, term-sheet evaluation, diligence, and closing coordination.

04 — Intelligence

A decision before a process.

Not every opportunity is ready to take to market. If the better answer is to resize, restructure, wait, or pursue a different capital path, that should be clear before time and credibility are spent.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out.

What types of mandates does Light Tower Group accept? +
Light Tower Group advises on debt and equity placements across commercial real estate, including multifamily, industrial, office, hospitality, mixed-use, self storage, student housing, and development. We are most useful where capital structure, execution risk, or timing requires more than a standard process.
Is there a minimum deal size? +
We typically engage on mandates of $5 million and above, though we evaluate each transaction on its own merits — including sponsor quality, asset type, and capital structure complexity. The right deal is the right deal regardless of size.
What capital sources does Light Tower Group access? +
Each assignment is assessed against the capital providers relevant to its asset type, structure, leverage, market, and timing. That can include banks, CMBS lenders, life insurance companies, agency lenders, debt funds, equity funds, family offices, and co-GP capital providers.
Do you charge a retainer or upfront fees? +
Fee structure is discussed after an initial assessment of the assignment and fit. The first conversation is confidential and focused on whether a targeted capital process is appropriate.
How does the process work from first conversation to close? +
It starts with a confidential conversation about the asset, capital need, objectives, and timeline. From there we assess the structure, define the capital strategy, prepare the underwriting case, and run a focused process with relevant sources. We remain engaged through funding.
What markets does Light Tower Group cover? +
We evaluate assignments across U.S. markets. The right execution path depends on the asset, sponsor, structure, and the capital providers active in that market at the time of the process.
