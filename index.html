Questions, Answered

A few practical questions, answered.

What types of mandates does Light Tower Group accept? +
Debt and equity assignments across commercial real estate, especially where structure, execution risk, or timing requires more than a standard process.
Is there a minimum deal size? +
We typically start at $5 million+ in capital needs, with fit evaluated based on the sponsor, asset, structure, and timeline.
What capital sources does Light Tower Group access? +
The providers relevant to the asset, structure, leverage, market, and timing—such as banks, debt funds, CMBS, life companies, agency lenders, equity funds, and family offices.
Do you charge a retainer or upfront fees? +
For qualifying assignments, engagement is success-based—no retainer or upfront fee. Terms are confirmed after an initial confidential review.
How does the process work from first conversation to close? +
We review the asset, capital need, objective, and timeline; define the strategy; prepare the case; and manage a focused process through funding.
What markets does Light Tower Group cover? +
We evaluate assignments across U.S. markets. The right path depends on the asset, sponsor, structure, and active capital providers.
