Common Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out.

What types of mandates does Light Tower Group accept? +
Light Tower Group works on debt and equity placements across all major commercial real estate asset classes — including multifamily, industrial, office, hospitality, mixed-use, self storage, student housing, and ground-up development. We specialize in complex mandates where standard execution falls short.
Is there a minimum deal size? +
We typically engage on mandates of $5 million and above, though we evaluate each transaction on its own merits — including sponsor quality, asset type, and capital structure complexity. The right deal is the right deal regardless of size.
What capital sources does Light Tower Group access? +
Our platform encompasses 250,000+ capital sources across the full spectrum: regional and national banks, CMBS lenders, life insurance companies, agency lenders (Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, FHA/HUD), debt funds, bridge lenders, construction lenders, institutional equity funds, family offices, private equity real estate firms, and co-GP capital providers.
Do you charge a retainer or upfront fees? +
No. Our compensation is fully success-based. We earn a fee at closing, not before. We believe in complete structural alignment with our sponsors — we are only successful when you close.
How does the process work from first conversation to close? +
It starts with a confidential consultation — typically 30 minutes — to understand your asset, capital need, and objectives. From there we build a capital markets strategy, prepare institutional-quality materials, and run a targeted process with the most relevant sources. We remain engaged through closing and funding, not just term sheet.
What markets does Light Tower Group cover? +
We work on mandates nationwide across all U.S. markets — primary, secondary, and tertiary. Our capital relationships are not geographically constrained, allowing us to place capital in markets where local brokers often have limited reach.
