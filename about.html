Principal & Founder

Ben Rohr

Light Tower Group  ·  New York

Ben Rohr is the Principal and Founder of Light Tower Group, a New York-based institutional capital advisory firm focused on commercial real estate debt and equity placement. He works with sponsors, developers, and operators on complex mandates — from ground-up construction and bridge financing to joint venture equity and preferred equity structuring — drawing on a platform of more than 250,000 capital sources across banks, debt funds, life companies, agency lenders, and institutional equity.

Light Tower Group operates with a principal-led model: Ben is directly engaged on every mandate from initial strategy through closing. The firm's coverage spans all major commercial real estate asset classes and U.S. markets, with particular depth in New York City multifamily, mixed-use, and development finance.

In addition to advisory work, Ben writes Light Tower Group's capital markets research — analytical coverage of significant NYC CRE transactions, financing trends, and market intelligence published to the firm's Insights feed.
