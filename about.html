Light Tower Group is a principal-led commercial real estate debt and equity advisory firm. Every mandate is run by Benjamin Rohr — from first call to closing.

Our Approach

Capital advisory is not a commodity. Every deal has a story — a business plan, a market thesis, a set of constraints that make it unique. Our job is to translate that story into the optimal capital structure and execute with precision.

We don't operate on volume. We operate on alignment. Each mandate receives the full weight of our platform, our relationships, and our expertise — because in capital markets, the difference between good execution and great execution is measured in millions.

Our capital source network spans institutional debt funds, regional banks, life insurance companies, CMBS lenders, agency platforms, family offices, and private equity firms. We don't have a preferred lender — we have the right lender for your deal.

"The best deals aren't won on rate. They're won on structure, speed, and certainty."

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of deals does Light Tower Group work on? +
Light Tower Group works on commercial real estate debt and equity mandates across all major asset classes — multifamily, office, retail, industrial, hospitality, and mixed-use. We handle the full capital stack: senior debt (permanent, bridge, construction), mezzanine, preferred equity, and JV equity. Deal sizes typically range from $5M to $100M+, and we are active in major markets nationwide.
How does Light Tower Group get paid? +
We earn an advisory fee upon successful placement of capital, paid at closing. We are fully aligned with our clients — we only get paid when you close. We do not charge retainers or upfront fees. Our fee structure is disclosed transparently at the beginning of every engagement.
How long does a typical capital raise take? +
Timeline depends on deal complexity, asset type, and market conditions. A straightforward bridge loan on a stabilized multifamily asset can close in 30–45 days. A construction financing or complex JV equity raise typically takes 60–120 days from mandate to closing. We manage the entire process — lender solicitation, term sheet negotiation, due diligence coordination, and closing — to compress timelines wherever possible.
Does Light Tower Group work with first-time sponsors? +
Yes, selectively. We evaluate sponsors holistically — track record matters, but so does business acumen, market knowledge, and the quality of the deal. First-time sponsors with a strong deal, a credible business plan, and the right equity can absolutely succeed in our process. We provide counsel on positioning and lender expectations as part of our advisory mandate.
What markets does Light Tower Group cover? +
We are active in all major U.S. markets: New York, Miami, Dallas, Atlanta, Chicago, Austin, Nashville, Los Angeles, Phoenix, and Charlotte. Our lender network is national, giving us the ability to source capital for deals in secondary and tertiary markets as well.
